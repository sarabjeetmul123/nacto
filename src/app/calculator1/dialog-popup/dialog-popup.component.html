Know more about HAS-BLED

Hypertension

  • Uncontrolled with systolic Blood Pressure >160 mmHg

Renal dysfunction

  • Chronic dialysis, renal transplantation, or serum creatinine ≥ 200 μmol/L

Liver dysfunction

  • Bilirubin >2x normal
  • Alkaline Phosphatase >3x normal
  • Liver transaminases (e.g. AST, ALT) >3x normal

Stroke history

Bleeding history

  • Major bleeding or predisposition
  • Bleeding that required hospitalization
  • Bleeding with serious consequences (e.g. Intracranial Hemorrhage)
  • Hemoglobin decrease of >2 g/dl
  • Bleeding that required transfusion

Labile INRs

  • History of over-Anticoagulation

Elderly

  • Age over 65 years old

Drugs (up to 1 point for meds and 1 point for Alcohol)

  • Other Anticoagulants
  • Anti-platelet agents
  • NSAIDs
  • Alcohol ≥8 drinks weekly
